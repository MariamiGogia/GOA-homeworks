The learning process

In Goa, classes are held online in the well-known application, Discord.
The duration of the lesson is 2 hours and the break is 10 minutes. Each group has a mentor who conducts
the lesson and absolutely all children are involved in the lesson. To explain, we use the application SoloLearn ( sololearn ).

Hackathon

In addition to teaching, hackathons are held in Goa, where we invite several groups.
We divide the children into groups and the competition begins. The group that wins will definitely be rewarded.
There is a dress code at the hackathon and it is necessary for the child to dress classically.

Junk food is not allowed at the hackathon.

Goaland

In addition to the hackathon, Goaland is also held, where the students of Goa gather and have fun.
There are fun activities as well as barbecuing, taking photos and meeting new people.
